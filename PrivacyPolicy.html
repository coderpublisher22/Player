Privacy Policy

AbMy Player - Your Privacy Matters

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Important: AbMy Player is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to AbMy Player ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our IPTV player application.

AbMy Player is a media player only. We do not provide, host, or distribute any IPTV content or services. You must provide your own IPTV service credentials.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • IPTV Credentials: Server URL, username, and password (stored locally on your device)
  • M3U Playlists: Playlist files you import (stored locally)
  • Preferences: App settings and preferences

2.2 Information We Automatically Collect

  • Usage Data: Favorites, watch history (stored locally)
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version (for compatibility)
  • App Analytics: Crash reports and performance data (anonymized)

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your personal identity information
  • We do NOT track your viewing habits
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT access your IPTV credentials (they stay on your device)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide IPTV playback features
  • Local Storage: To save your favorites and watch history
  • App Improvement: To fix bugs and improve performance
  • Support: To respond to your support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • IPTV credentials are encrypted and stored in secure device storage
  • Favorites and watch history are stored in local database
  • No data is transmitted to our servers

4.2 Security Measures

  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure storage using iOS Keychain
  • No cloud backup of credentials
  • HTTPS connections for network requests

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Advertising (if enabled)

If you see ads in the app, we use Google AdMob which may collect:

  • Device advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • App usage for ad targeting
  • You can opt-out via iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking

5.2 Analytics

We may use analytics services to improve the app:

  • Anonymized crash reports
  • App performance metrics
  • No personally identifiable information

6. Your IPTV Service Provider

Important: When you use AbMy Player, you connect directly to your IPTV service provider's servers. Their privacy policy applies to:

  • Content streaming and viewing data
  • Your subscription information
  • Any data they collect during streaming

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the privacy practices of your IPTV provider.

7. Children's Privacy

AbMy Player is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app
  • Delete: Remove all app data by deleting the app
  • Opt-Out: Disable ad tracking in iOS Settings
  • Export: Export your favorites and playlists

9. Data Retention

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can clear data anytime in Settings
  • No data is retained on our servers

10. International Users

AbMy Player is available worldwide. Your data stays on your device regardless of your location. If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing an in-app notification for significant changes
