Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I add my streaming subscription?
Go to Settings → Add Account → Enter your streaming provider's server URL, username, and password. The app supports Xtream Codes API and M3U playlist URLs.
❓ Does AbMy Player provide streaming subscriptions?
No, AbMy Player is a media player only. You must have your own streaming subscription from a legal provider. We do not sell or provide streaming services.
❓ Why can't I play some channels?
This may be due to: (1) Server issues from your streaming provider, (2) Expired subscription, (3) Network connectivity problems, or (4) Geographic restrictions. Contact your streaming provider for content-related issues.
❓ How do I import an M3U playlist?
Go to Settings → Add Account → Select M3U URL or M3U File → Enter the URL or select a file from your device. The playlist will be imported automatically.
❓ Is my data safe and private?
Yes! All your credentials and data are stored locally on your device with AES-256 encryption. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
❓ Can I use AbMy Player on multiple devices?
Yes, you can install AbMy Player on multiple devices (iPhone, iPad). However, check with your streaming provider about their concurrent stream limits.
❓ The app is crashing or freezing. What should I do?
Try these steps: (1) Force quit and restart the app, (2) Clear app cache in Settings, (3) Update to the latest version, (4) Restart your device. If the issue persists, email us with details about your device and iOS version.
❓ Do you support catch-up TV (EPG)?
Yes, if your streaming provider offers EPG data and catch-up functionality, it will work automatically in AbMy Player.
❓ Is there a limit to favorites or playlists?
No, you can add unlimited favorites and channels. The only limit is your device's storage capacity.
❓ How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email us at support@najfes.me with: (1) Bug description or feature request, (2) Your device model and iOS version, (3) App version (found in Settings → About), (4) Screenshots if applicable.

What to Include in Your Support Email

To help us assist you faster, please include:

  • Clear description of the issue
  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro, iPad Air)
  • iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • App version (Settings → About in AbMy Player)
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue

⚠️ Important: For issues related to your streaming service (missing channels, login problems, content quality), please contact your streaming provider directly. We cannot assist with third-party service issues.
